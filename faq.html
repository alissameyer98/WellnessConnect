Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How can I join local wellness events?

You can check our Events page for upcoming activities and RSVP or join the Facebook group for real-time updates!

Is Wellness Connect free to join?

Yes! All events and activities listed are free or low-cost unless otherwise noted. Joining the community is free.

How do I host an event?

If you'd like to host a wellness event, simply reach out through our Contact page. We'd love to help you organize it!

How do I stay updated?

Subscribe to our email list on the Subscribe page to get the latest updates, tips, and events directly to your inbox.
