About Us

Welcome to Wellness Connect – a community created to help young women find meaningful connections through wellness activities. Our mission is to bridge the gap between college and the professional world by offering a platform where women can discover local events and foster a sense of belonging.

Wellness Connect provides a space where women can explore a variety of wellness activities, like group hikes, outdoor fitness classes, farmers’ market hangs, and coffee meetups. Our platform features an updated calendar of local events and offerings, as well as chats for users to foster community opportunities themselves!

Our goal is simple: to create a low-pressure environment where young women can explore wellness, improve mental and physical health, and build lasting friendships. Whether you’re new to an area or just looking for a way to engage with your local community, Wellness Connect is here to help you feel supported and inspired.
